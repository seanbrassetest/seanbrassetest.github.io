Pokemon Information

Pikachu

  • Electric type
  • First appeared in the Pokemon Red and Green video games
  • Known for its lightning bolt-shaped tail and cute appearance

Bulbasaur

  • Grass/Poison type
  • One of the original three starter Pokemon
  • Has a bulb on its back that grows as it absorbs sunlight

Squirtle

  • Water type
  • One of the original three starter Pokemon
  • Has a shell and can shoot water from its mouth

Charmander

  • Fire type
  • One of the original three starter Pokemon
  • Has a flame on its tail and can breathe fire
